About me!

I am a junior at Cal State University, Monterey Bay.

I started coding at 15 years old in Monterey Trail High School's Design and Technology Academy (DATA).

Now in college, I've grown a lot as a Software Engineer, always finding opportunities to become a better SWE.

I am in CSUMB's CS++ Cohort, a four-year program geared to better prepare CS students for the Software Engineering industry.

I've participated in Google's Computer Science Summer Institute (CSSI) and Capital One's Software Engineering Summit.

With the knowledge from those experiences, classes, and my own endeavors to learn about Software Engineering, I was able to receive the opportunity to be a Software Engineer Intern for Uber!

With industry experience (and the excitement of seeing my work on the ACTUAL UBEREATS APP), I am more confident than ever as an engineer and I am still striving to learn the most that I possibly can.

I want to continue to work on mobile development, and my goal is to release some type of mobile app before I graduate!
